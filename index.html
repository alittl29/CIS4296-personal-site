Projects

I've worked on some neat things.
URP/SSP Success Tracker

For the Director of the Undergraduate Research Program who needs a more efficient way to keep track of student research success. The URP/SSP Student Research Success Tracker is a system that receives records of all past and current URP/SSP students, and allows the students to update their student success records. Different from the current process of manually entering data into an excel sheet, our system will allow the students to easily update their records to be reviewed by the Dean’s office in a timely fashion.

FRAMES: Fighting Game Companion App

For fighting game players of all skill levels, who have trouble keeping track of multiple sources of fighting game information or wish to get into the genre and don’t know where to begin, the “FRAMES Fighting Game Companion App” is a Mobile Application that Allows users to quickly lookup, store, and share information about competitive fighting games. Unlike The current method of searching the internet for multiple guides, videos, and blogs containing information, our product is a centralized hub to store the knowledge of multiple games in an easy to use application that can be updated and modified to always be up to date.
